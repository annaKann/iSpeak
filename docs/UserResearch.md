# User Research Observations

## Observation 1

- **Observation Statement:**  
The majority of users said they want athlete voices for competitive motivation.

- **Supporting Data:**  
In the focus group survey, 38% of respondents selected the option indicating they preferred motivational messages from professional athletes. The next closest was 27% for calm mentors.

- **Team Action (if any):**  
Ensure professional athlete's voices are included in our available list.
